Our Story

Same Day Dent Repair is a family owned and operated autobody repair service, serving North Texas.

With over 20 years of experience in the autobody repair industry. Specializing in paintless dent removal, bumper dent removal, touch-up paint, and part replacement.

We provide a same day mobile service which allows us to pass the savings on to you, the customer. We can save you around 50% of the cost from a conventional bodyshop and most jobs are done the same day, hence the name Same Day Dent Repair.
